sq mile        sq km island of Niihau neeeehow is called The Forbidden        Island because it is privately owned and cannot be visited without an        invitation from one of the  inhabitants Most the tiny Niihau seashells into leis that fetch big        prices There are no hotels on Niihau  nor electricity or running        water Once they finish the lower grades the children of Niihau attend
