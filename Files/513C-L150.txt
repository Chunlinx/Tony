              Dear IUSD Colleague      Indiana University and the health profession suffered a significant      loss with the passing of Dr Steven L Bricker on March   A      nationally recognized innovator in Oral Diagnosis and Oral Medicine      Steve is remembered as a gentleman whose care and concern for his      students friends and colleagues was warm and genuine Dr Bricker      pursued excellence for his department university and profession with      unending determination As Chair of Diagnostic Sciences for Indiana      University School of Dentistry Steve Bricker set a standard for      excellence to which we all aspire      We have created a scholarship endowment which will permanently      recognize Dr Steven Brickers contributions to our school and      profession The Bricker Scholarship will provide support for students      engaged in graduate and postgraduate work in the diagnostic sciences at      our dental school This endowment is a fitting tribute to a man who made      a difference and will realize one of Steves passions  supporting      students      The outcry from across the country over the loss of Steve Bricker has      been significant People here at the school have asked for the      opportunity to support students and recognize his good work by      participating in the Steven L Bricker Scholarship Endowment You may use      the attached form if you wish to make a gift Your contribution made      through Indiana University Foundation is taxdeductible      Sincerely      STEVEN L BRICKER SCHOLARSHIP ENDOWMENT
