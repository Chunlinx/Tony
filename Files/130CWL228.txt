              Sudden Infant Death Syndrome commonly known as crib death is our      nations major cause of death for infants one week to one year of age      Annually SIDS claims the lives of approximately  babies in the      United States It is one of the more significant factors that places the      United States th among the  industrialized nations of the world in      infant mortality rates SIDS is a public health issue of utmost concern      to every new and expectant parentand grandparentin the United States      today      Although research is beginning to provide answers regarding factors      that may increase the risk for SIDS and suggest actions parents can take      to reduce the risks these measures will not save all babies      We still do not know what causes SIDS we cannot predict which babies      are vulnerable nor can we prevent all SIDS deaths Further research is      needed and families still need help      The SIDS Alliance is a nonprofit tax exempt national voluntary      health organization established over a decade ago to combat SIDS The      SIDS Alliance and its  Affiliates throughout the United States are      dedicated to eliminating SIDS through research serving SIDS families      and educating professionals and the general public about SIDS The SIDS      Alliance meets all National Charities Information Bureau NCIB      standards and is listed in the NCIB Wisegiving Guide      The SIDS Alliance has also received a high rating A from the      American Institute of Philanthropy and has been named one of the top      childrens charities in America by Parenting Child and Parents      magazines      Until our future is free from SIDS the SIDS Alliance will continue to      serve as a source of strength for the tens of thousands of families who      have experienced the loss of a beloved baby to SIDS an advocate for      compassion and public understanding and a catalyst for finding solutions      through research
